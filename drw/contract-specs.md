# DRW Call Your Shot 

## Contract Set 1: Binary Team Performance
A contract for each team which will settle 100 for the winning team and 0 for all other participants.

Price Range: 0 - 100

Max position size:  100

P&L Threshold: -500,000 (if your P&L dips below this number, you can only enter orders that reduce your position)

## Contract Set 2: Points Based Team Team Performance
A contract for each team with points allocated based on how far each participating team advances in the tournament.

Settlement of each team will be as follows:

64 for champion

32 for runner up – losing in the Finals

24 for winning the Bronze Final (semi-finals)

16 for losing in the Bronze Final (semi-finals)

8 for losing in the Quarterfinals

4 for losing in the Round of 16

2 for losing in the Round of 32

0 for not progressing from the Group Stage

Tradeable Price Range: 0 - 64

Max Position Size: 100

P&L Threshold: -300,000

## Contract Set 3: Binary Team Performance
A contract for each team which will settle based on the the accumulated goals scored over the course of the entire tournament – both Group Play and Knockouts - by that team.

Tradeable Price Range: 0 - 100

Max Position Size: 100

P&L Threshold: -100,000
